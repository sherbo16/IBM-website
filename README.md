# Health-Wise AI 🚀
> An AI-Integrated Healthcare & Mental Wellness Assistant

Health-Wise AI is an all-in-one AI-powered healthcare assistant that bridges the data gap between patient care and medical professionals. It provides real-time physical triage alongside continuous mental wellness tracking to act as a proactive digital safety net.

---

## 🛠️ Product UI & Architecture

### 1. Physical Healthcare Triage Layer
Users can access instant medical screening and diagnostics directly from the primary panel.

* **AI Symptom Checker:** Users describe physical discomfort in natural language; AI evaluates and predicts primary diagnostic insights.
* **Medical Report Simplifier:** Upload lab reports, and the AI strips away complex medical jargons to provide an easy-to-understand summary.
* **Medicine Guide:** Tracks prescribed tablet timings, custom dosage alerts, and potential side effects to eliminate medical errors.

![Physical Triage Layer](1000189387.jpg)

### 2. Mental Wellness & Holistic Health Layer
Continuous engagement algorithms focused on preventive lifestyle corrections and mental safety.

* **AI Mental Wellness Chatbot:** Live text and voice analysis for real-time stress relief, constant motivation, and personalized mindfulness routines.
* **Nutrition & Fitness Planner:** Dynamic AI-generated meal plans and home-workout schedules based on individual health goals.
* **Dynamic SOS Trigger:** A prominent SOS button that triggers geolocated alerts and critical vital data to mapped hospital networks and emergency contacts.
